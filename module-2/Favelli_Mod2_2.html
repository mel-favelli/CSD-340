My name is Melissa and I am 31 years old. I currently reside in Madison, Wisconsin with my partner, David, and our two siberian huskies, Juneau and Bear. I moved to Madison a couple months after graduating high school, which was about 14 years ago. I moved here from Salem, Wisconsin, which is a small farm town just north of the Wisconsin-Illinois border. There wasn't much going on in Salem besides the fact that my parents and the few friends that I had lived there, but many friends were going off to college and doing their own thing.
